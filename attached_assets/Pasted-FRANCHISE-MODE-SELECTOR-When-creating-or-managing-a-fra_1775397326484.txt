FRANCHISE MODE SELECTOR: When creating or managing a franchise, the commissioner can choose between two modes. SOLO GM MODE — one person has full control of the entire franchise, all roster moves, trades, draft, depth chart, game planning, no invite codes needed. CO-GM MODE — multiple GMs share full access to the franchise simultaneously, commissioner sends invite codes to brothers, all changes sync in real time through Supabase, any Co-GM can be removed and the franchise continues. The commissioner can switch between Solo and Co-GM mode at any time from settings without losing any data. If switching from Co-GM to Solo, other GMs lose access but all their moves and changes are preserved. If switching from Solo to Co-GM, commissioner can generate a new invite code and bring brothers in at any point mid-season or during offseason. Show clearly on the home dashboard which mode is active — Solo GM or Co-GM with how many active GMs are connected.
CONTINUOUS FRANCHISE: The franchise never resets. All data persists across every season indefinitely — full season by season history, every transaction ever made, every game result, every stat from every season. Users can browse franchise history year by year going back to season 1.
HALL OF FAME: After each season eligible retired players are evaluated for the VFL Hall of Fame based on career stats, Pro Bowl selections, championships won, season records broken, and overall impact. A voting system determines inductees each offseason. Hall of Fame players get a special gold HOF card, a permanent place in the franchise Hall of Fame gallery, and their retired number if they were a franchise legend. Show their career stats, accolades, championships, and seasons played.
AWARDS SYSTEM: After every season generate full awards — VFL MVP, Offensive Player of the Year, Defensive Player of the Year, Rookie of the Year, Coach of the Year, Comeback Player of the Year, Offensive and Defensive lineman of the year. Show an awards ceremony screen with the winner and runner ups and their stats.
CHAMPIONSHIPS: Track every VFL Championship winner across all seasons. Show a championship banner history in the franchise hub. Players who win championships get a ring icon on their card forever. Track dynasty runs — any franchise winning multiple championships gets dynasty status.”
